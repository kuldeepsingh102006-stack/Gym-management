About GymPro

Why we built a gym management platform, and what it's meant to solve.

Our Story

Most small and mid-size gyms still run on registers, WhatsApp groups and spreadsheets to track members, payments and stock. GymPro started as a simple idea - put all of that in one place so gym owners spend less time on admin and more time actually running the gym floor.

What We Do

GymPro gives gym owners a single dashboard to manage memberships, track renewals, sell products at the front desk, and keep an eye on every branch if they run more than one location. It's built to be simple enough for a front desk staff member to use on day one.

Our Mission

Make gym management simple for owners who don't have time to learn complicated software.

Who We Serve

Independent gyms, fitness studios and small chains looking to move away from manual tracking.

Where We're Headed

Adding class scheduling and trainer management as the platform grows.
